Graphics Cards
A graphics card in a gaming PC powers the visuals, creating smooth, detailed, and immersive scenes in your favorite games. It ensures high performance and responsiveness so you can enjoy fast, fluid gameplay at the best quality settings.
Processors
A CPU is the main processor in a gaming PC, handling all the core tasks and keeping your system running fast and responsive. It works behind the scenes to manage game logic, controls, and performance so everything runs smoothly while you play.
RAM
RAM is your computers short-term memory, helping it quickly access the information it needs while you play or multitask. More RAM allows for smoother gameplay, faster switching between tasks, and better overall performance.
Storage
An NVMe SSD is a super-fast storage drive that loads games, levels, and applications in seconds. It keeps your system feeling quick and responsive by dramatically reducing waiting times.
